NUNGU BUSINESS SCHOOL

PROFESSIONAL COURSES/ OPEN ENROLMENT PROGRAMS

We teach you risk-taking and boundary-breaking entrepreneurial and managerial skills, we’re a school that teaches with Africans in mind while we looks to the future. Nungu Business School provides open enrolment and open graded courses to aid you and make you a better entrepreneur or manager, our courses draw on market trends across Africa to provide fresh out-of-the-box education using innovative technologies.

Learn More

Nungu Business School offers a unique and highly engaging way to learn vital business concepts. Our programs are designed to bring the Nungu Business School continental experience to you, and are built around three key characteristics:

  •  Vigorous
  •  Case-based learning
  •  Group

Nungu Business School can create custom programs tailored to meet you or your organization’s specific needs in Leadership. Negotiation, Marketing, Ethics, Supply Chain Management, Finance, Accounting, Entrepreneurship, Management, Project Management, Production Management, Selling, Human Capital development, Communication, ICT and a host of others . We will create personalized programs for high ranking government officials, business executives, and governors of states ranging from one week to several months.

What Sets NBS Online Apart?

  • VIGOROUS

    NBS Online courses are nothing like a typical sit-back-and-listen lecture. You’ll engage in a new activity every three to five minutes—think polls, problem-solving exercises, and short essays. You will be asked at random to concisely state your position on an issue in a matter of minutes. Each element is designed to keep you interested, involved, and on your toes.

  • CASE BASED

    Submerse yourself in real-world learning. Lessons are brought to life through cases, which put you in the driver seat of business leaders. Hear directly, the challenges they faced, and contend with the same issues and imperfect information. As you discuss and debate solutions with your colleagues, you will learn vital business thoughts you can apply in your own career.

  • GROUP

    Engaging with your classmate is a big part of what makes NBS Online unique. You exchange thoughts, offer ideas, and seek out viewpoints from a community of learners gathered from around the world. In the process, you learn from each other’s experiences and viewpoints, and leave the course with a larger outlook on the world.
